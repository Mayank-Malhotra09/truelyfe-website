Frequently Asked Questions

Is TrueLyfe natural or processed?

TrueLyfe is made from real whole cow milk. Ultra-filtration and retort sterilization are physical processes — no chemicals, no additives, no powders.

Why does it have 2X more protein?

Ultra-filtration concentrates natural protein from real cow milk. No protein powder is added at any stage.

Does 90-day shelf life mean chemicals are added?

No. Shelf life comes from batch retort sterilization — the same technology used for baby food globally. Zero preservatives.

Will it taste different from regular milk?

Slightly richer and creamier due to higher protein. Works perfectly in chai, smoothies, and cooking.

Who is the Lactose-Free variant for?

Anyone who experiences bloating after regular milk. Lactose is broken down naturally using lactase enzyme.
